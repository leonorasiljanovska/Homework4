Our Mission

We are a passionate team of three software engineering students dedicated to creating innovative solutions for the financial world. Our project focuses on providing insightful predictions for the Macedonian Stock Exchange to help investors make informed decisions. As students from Macedonia, we wanted to make a real impact in our home country’s financial market by offering advanced tools for stock prediction, tailored to the unique needs of the Macedonian Stock Exchange.
